A TV Show Adaptation

Set in Utopian Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun, the story follows the origins of two iconic League Of Legends champions and the power that will tear them apart.

The delicate balance between the rich city of Piltover and the seedy underbelly of Zaun. Tensions between these city-states boil over with the creation of hextech a way for any person to control magical energy in Piltover, and in Zaun, a new drug called shimmer transforms humans into monsters. The rivalry between the cities splits families and friends as Arcane brings life to the relationships that shape some of League of Legends' famous champions including Vi, Jinx, Caitlyn, Jayce and Viktor.

In the cities of Piltover and Zaun, tensions rise as inventors, hooligans, politicians and crime lords grow increasingly fed up with the constraints of a devastated society. With the situation on the verge of being unsustainable, two sisters steal an artifact of immeasurable power. Discovery and danger collide as heroes are born and bonds are broken. Will this power change the world? Or will it lead to ruin? This is the world of Arcane."

ARC I - EPISODE 1

Two sisters, Jinx and Vi, grew up in the oppressed underbelly of Piltover, a city that runs on hextech. After an accident that leaves them orphaned, the sisters must navigate their relationship while finding their place in a world divided by class and conflict.

ARC I - EPISODE 2

Jinx seeks revenge against the enforcers of Piltover, while Vi tries to stop a dangerous new drug from spreading in the underbelly. As their paths begin to cross once again, the sisters are forced to confront the ghosts of their past and the realities of their present.

ARC I - EPISODE 3

Jinx and her accomplices plan a heist to steal a powerful hextech weapon, while Vi tries to track down the source of the drug that is wreaking havoc in the underbelly. As the sisters' paths begin to converge once again, they must confront their conflicting loyalties and decide where their allegiances lie.

ARC II - EPISODE 4

Jinx struggles with the consequences of her actions as she comes face-to-face with the people she has hurt. Meanwhile, Vi continues her investigation into the source of the drug, and uncovers a surprising connection to her own past.

ARC II - EPISODE 5

Jinx and Vi confront their past traumas as they face off against a powerful enemy. With the help of unexpected allies, they must find a way to survive in a world that seems determined to tear them apart.

ARC II - EPISODE 6

Jinx and Vi must make a difficult decision as they face the consequences of their actions. As their world is turned upside down, they must come to terms with their past and their future, and find a way to move forward together.

ARC III - EPISODE 7

Jinx and Vi's bond is put to the test as they face their toughest challenge yet. As they fight to protect their loved ones and their city,they must confront their deepest fears and make sacrifices for the greater good.

ARC III - EPISODE 8

The past and present collide as the true nature of the conflict between Piltover and the underbelly is revealed. Jinx and Vi must choose their sides and fight for what they believe in, as the fate of their world hangs in the balance.

ARC III - EPISODE 9

Jinx and Vi face off in a final showdown that will determine the future of their world. As their bond is tested to the limit, they must confront the consequences of their actions and make a decision that will change their lives forever.
